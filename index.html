About Our College

A school community committed to knowledge, service, and progress.

Why Choose GGCAST?

GGCAST provides students with a strong educational foundation through relevant academic programs, experienced faculty, and student-centered learning.

We focus on developing not only academic competence but also leadership, discipline, creativity, and social responsibility.

Our institution aims to prepare graduates who are ready for employment, lifelong learning, and meaningful service to society.

Quality Education

Driven by excellence, innovation, and community values.

Mission and Vision

Guided by excellence, service, and values-driven education.

Our Vision

A higher learning institution equipped with competent human resources, state-of-the-art facilities, academic standards, and has disaster resiliency, essential to the development of globally competitive economically stable, socially involved, environmentally-friendly, and spiritually molded individuals for a highly progressive community.

Our Mission

The college shall provide quality education to all by producing competent graduates and cultivating individuals with deep sense of values in life, vibrant leadership, proficient entrepreneurial skills, strong environmental advocacy, and multi-cultural sensitivity through excellent instruction, robust research, active community service and involvement.

Enrollment Requirements

Please prepare the following documents for enrollment.

For Freshmen

Required Documents

  • Certificate of Transfer (Honorable Dismissal)
  • Certified True Copy of Grades or Transcript of Record
  • Barangay Clearance
  • PSA Birth Certificate (original copy)
  • Cedula (18 years old and above)
  • 2 pcs. each 1x1 and 2x2 ID with white background
  • If married: PSA Marriage Certificate (original copy)
  • Long brown envelope

Bring original and complete requirements to avoid delays in processing your enrollment.

For Old Students

Required Documents

  • Prospectus
  • School ID
  • Student Clearance

Old students are advised to bring all required documents for a smooth re-enrollment process.
